Isambard Kingdom Brunel

A Great British Engineer

A symbol of Britain at its best

Isambard Kingdom Brunel is probably the best known British Engineer. Brunel lived during a period of great prosperity and optimism. A period following on from the industrial revolution. It was a period of great inovation and experimentation during the reign of Queen Victoria when the British Empire looked out on the world like a bright beacon.

Brunel was involved in some of the most important engineering works during his time. Today his most famous work, the Clifton Suspension Bridge, is still in daily use and is a testemony to the life and times of its chief engineer.

Isambard Kingdom Brunel is a symbol of Great Britain at its best and most optimistic and a continuing inspiration to all young and aspiring engineers.
